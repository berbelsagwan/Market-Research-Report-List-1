<p><p>The marine shaft power meter market is categorized into digital and analog display types. Digital display meters utilize electronic components to provide precise, real-time readings of shaft power, enhancing accuracy and ease of interpretation. They often feature advanced data logging capabilities and user-friendly interfaces. In contrast, analog display meters rely on traditional needle gauges to indicate power levels, offering simplicity and reliability. While digital meters are popular for their technological advancements, analog meters are valued for their straightforward operation and lower maintenance needs.</p></p>
