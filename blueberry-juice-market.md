<p><p>The blueberry juice market consists of three main types: concentrate, dilute juice, and compound juice. Concentrate is made by removing water from the juice, resulting in a thicker product that has a longer shelf life. Dilute juice is often sold in a ready-to-drink form, requiring little or no preparation. Compound juice combines blueberry juice with other fruit juices or flavors to create a unique blend. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios, from direct consumption to mixing in beverages.</p></p>
