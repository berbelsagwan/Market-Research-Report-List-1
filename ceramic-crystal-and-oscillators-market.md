<p><p>The ceramic crystal and oscillator market is primarily divided into two types: through-hole and surface mount. Through-hole oscillators are designed for insertion into a PCB with leads that pass through the board, providing robust mechanical support, typically used in traditional electronics. Surface mount oscillators, on the other hand, are compact components soldered directly onto the surface of a PCB, allowing for a more space-efficient design. Both types cater to various applications across consumer electronics, telecommunications, and industrial systems.</p></p>
