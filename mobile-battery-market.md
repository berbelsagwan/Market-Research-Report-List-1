<p><p>The mobile battery market encompasses various types categorized by their chemical compositions. Nickel-based batteries, such as Nickel-Cobalt-Aluminum (NCA), are known for high energy density but can pose safety risks. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies, providing diverse options for mobile energy solutions.</p></p>
