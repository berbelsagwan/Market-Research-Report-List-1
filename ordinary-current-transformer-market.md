<p><p>The Ordinary Current Transformer (OCT) market can be segmented into primary and secondary winding types based on their functionality. Primary winding transformers directly carry the main current, providing a real-time measurement of current flow, ideal for high voltage applications. In contrast, secondary winding transformers operate with reduced currents, offering isolation and safety for measurement devices. Both types serve essential roles in electrical systems, ensuring accurate current monitoring and protection, catering to various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
