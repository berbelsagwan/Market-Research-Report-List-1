<p><p>The commercial aircraft landing gear and undercarriage market encompasses various types designed to support aircraft during landing and takeoff. Strut landing gear employs vertical supports to absorb shock, while rocker gear features pivoting arms for enhanced stability. Framed gear consists of a robust structure that distributes weight effectively, ensuring durability. Meanwhile, pontoon landing gear is utilized for seaplanes, designed to float on water, providing versatility for amphibious operations. Each type serves specific operational needs and enhances aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
