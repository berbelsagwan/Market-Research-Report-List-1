<p><p>A Passive Intermodulation Tester is a specialized device used to measure and analyze passive intermodulation (PIM) levels in communication systems. PIM occurs when non-linearities in passive components, such as antennas, connectors, or cables, generate unwanted signals due to the interaction of two or more frequencies. These testers help ensure optimal system performance by identifying and mitigating PIM, which can degrade signal quality and lead to network inefficiencies.</p><p>The Passive Intermodulation Tester Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for efficient communication networks, particularly with the rollout of 5G technology and the expansion of wireless communication infrastructure.
