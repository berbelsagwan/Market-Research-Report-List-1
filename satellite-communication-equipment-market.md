SATCOM antennas facilitate the transmission and reception of signals, and SATCOM modems/routers enable data processing and network connectivity. Additional equipment may include amplifiers, controllers, and terminals, contributing to diverse applications in telecommunications, broadcasting, and data services across various sectors.</p></p>
