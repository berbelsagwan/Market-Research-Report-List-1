<p><p>The single-phase isolation transformer market comprises two primary types: step-up and step-down transformers. Step-up transformers increase voltage from a lower level to a higher level, making them suitable for applications requiring voltage boosting, such as power distribution. Conversely, step-down transformers reduce voltage from a higher to a lower level, ideal for supplying power to end-user devices safely. Both types enhance electrical safety and system efficiency in residential, commercial, and industrial applications by isolating different circuits.</p></p>
