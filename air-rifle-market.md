<p><p>The air rifle market consists of various types catering to different preferences. Spring air rifles use a coiled spring for power, while gas piston air rifles utilize a gas-filled cylinder for smoother operation. Precharged pneumatic (PCP) air rifles offer high pressure for consistent shots, and CO2 powered air rifles use carbon dioxide cartridges for convenience. Variable pump air rifles allow users to manually pump for adjustable power. Each type targets specific needs in accuracy, ease of use, and shooting experience.</p></p>
