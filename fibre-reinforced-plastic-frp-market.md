<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) is a composite material made of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibers, offering high strength, lightweight, and corrosion resistance. The FRP market can be categorized into two main types: PAN-based and pitch-based. PAN-based FRP uses polyacrylonitrile fibers, known for their excellent tensile strength and thermal stability, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, pitch-based FRP employs pitch fibers, which provide exceptional thermal and electrical conductivity, suitable for specialized applications like energy and electronics.</p></p>
