<p><p>The dust removal equipment market features key technologies like electrostatic precipitators (ESPs) and bag filter machines, both vital for industrial pollution control. ESPs utilize electric charges to capture particulate matter from exhaust gases, making them efficient for large-scale operations. In contrast, bag filter machines employ fabric filters to trap dust and particulate matter, offering flexibility for varying air volumes. Both systems enhance air quality by reducing emissions, catering to industries such as manufacturing, power generation, and waste management.</p></p>
