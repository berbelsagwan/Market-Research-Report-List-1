<p><p>The organic milk powder market encompasses various types, including skimmed milk, whole milk, buttermilk, and whey. Skimmed milk powder has reduced fat content, making it ideal for low-calorie diets. Whole milk powder retains full fat, providing a creamy texture and rich flavor. Buttermilk powder is derived from the fermentation of whole milk, offering tanginess for baking and cooking. Whey powder, a byproduct of cheese production, is high in protein and popular in nutritional supplements. Each type serves distinct culinary and nutritional needs.</p></p>
