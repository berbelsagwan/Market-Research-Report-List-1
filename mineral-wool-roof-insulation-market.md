<p><p>The mineral wool roof insulation market is predominantly divided into two types: rockwool and glass wool insulation. Rockwool, derived from volcanic rock, offers excellent thermal and acoustic properties, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Glass wool, made from recycled glass, is lightweight and provides effective thermal insulation, often used in residential and commercial buildings. Both types are resistant to moisture and fire, contributing to energy efficiency and durability in roofing systems, catering to diverse construction needs and sustainability goals.</p></p>
