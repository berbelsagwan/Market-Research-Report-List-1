<p><p>The fruit juice beverage stabilizers market includes various types such as Xanthan Gum, Carrageenan, Gum Arabic, and Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC), among others. Xanthan Gum is a polysaccharide known for its thickening and stabilizing properties. Carrageenan, derived from seaweed, is used to enhance texture and prevent separation. Gum Arabic serves as an emulsifier and thickener. CMC, a cellulose derivative, provides viscosity and stabilization. These stabilizers improve the quality, shelf life, and consumer appeal of fruit juice beverages.</p></p>
