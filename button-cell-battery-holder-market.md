<p><strong>The Button Cell Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The button cell battery holder market can be categorized into two main types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount holders are designed for compact applications, allowing easy integration onto printed circuit boards (PCBs) with minimal height. They provide strong mechanical support and facilitate automated assembly. In contrast, through hole holders involve inserting leads into drilled holes in the PCB, ensuring better durability and stronger connections, making them ideal for high-reliability applications. Both types serve distinct industry needs, driven by design requirements.</p></p>
