<p><p>The aerospace high-performance alloys market encompasses three primary types: iron-based, cobalt-based, and nickel-based alloys. Iron-based alloys are known for their strength and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for structural components. Cobalt-based alloys offer excellent high-temperature stability and corrosion resistance, ideal for turbine and jet engine applications.
