<p><p>The gel pen market is segmented into two primary types: capped and retractable pens. Capped gel pens feature a removable cap that protects the nib, preventing drying and ensuring longevity. They are often favored for their variety of designs and colors. Retractable gel pens, on the other hand, have a click mechanism that retracts the tip, offering convenience and ease of use. This second type is popular for everyday writing tasks due to its practicality and reduced risk of ink drying out.</p></p>
