<p><p>The outdoor smart locker market is divided into two main types: contactless and contact. Contactless lockers enable users to access their packages remotely via mobile apps or QR codes, promoting convenience and minimizing physical interaction. These systems are especially appealing in the wake of health concerns. In contrast, contact lockers require users to physically interact with the unit, often involving direct handling of keys or combinations. Both types cater to unique consumer preferences and operational needs, enhancing parcel delivery efficiency.</p></p>
