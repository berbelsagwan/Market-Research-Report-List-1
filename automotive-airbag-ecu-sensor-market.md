<p><p>The automotive airbag ECU sensor market primarily comprises mass type sensors and roller type sensors. Mass type sensors detect changes in acceleration and deceleration, responding to impacts by deploying airbags to protect passengers. In contrast, roller type sensors use a pendulum or roller mechanism to measure vehicle motion and orientation, activating airbags in specific crash scenarios. These sensors enhance vehicle safety by ensuring timely deployment, thereby reducing injury risks during accidents and contributing to overall automotive safety systems.</p></p>
