<p><p>The Power Electronic Transformer (PET) market can be categorized into three types based on conversion stages. Single-stage transformers perform direct power conversion, typically seen in low-power applications. Two-stage transformers involve an intermediate energy conversion step, improving efficiency and adaptability for medium-power needs. Three-stage transformers integrate additional conversion processes, allowing for enhanced voltage regulation and functionality, making them suitable for high-power applications. Each type addresses specific requirements in energy management, efficiency, and application flexibility.</p></p>
