<p><p>Seismic Base Isolation is a technology designed to protect structures from earthquake forces by decoupling the building from ground motion. <p><p>The seismic base isolation market includes various types designed to enhance building resilience against earthquakes. Elastomeric isolation systems use rubber bearings to absorb seismic energy, providing flexibility and stability. Sliding isolation systems incorporate sliding surfaces that allow buildings to move independently of ground motion, reducing force transmission. Hybrid isolation systems combine both elastomeric and sliding technologies, optimizing performance under diverse seismic conditions.
