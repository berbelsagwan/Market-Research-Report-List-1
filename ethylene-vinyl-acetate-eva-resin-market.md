<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resin market is categorized by varying vinyl acetate content, influencing properties and applications. Very Low EVA contains less than 10% vinyl acetate, offering high flexibility and low temperature resilience, ideal for adhesives and coatings. Low EVA (10-20%) provides moderate flexibility, suited for films and packaging. Mid EVA (20-40%) balances strength and flexibility, used in automotive and consumer products, while High EVA (above 40%) excels in elasticity and is perfect for cushioning and specialty foams.</p></p>
