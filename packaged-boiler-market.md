<p><p>The packaged boiler market comprises various types, primarily shell boilers and drum boilers. Shell boilers are compact units that provide efficient steam generation, often used in smaller industrial applications. They feature a cylindrical shell containing water and steam, allowing for rapid heating. Drum boilers, on the other hand, are larger systems designed for higher capacity operations. They consist of a steam drum and a water drum, facilitating better steam separation and enhanced thermal efficiency, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes.</p></p>
