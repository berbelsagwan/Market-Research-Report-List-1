<p><p>A Vortex Flow Controller is an advanced device used to measure the flow rate of gases and liquids through the principle of vortex shedding. As fluids pass through a bluff body, vortices are generated, and the frequency of these vortices is directly proportional to the flow rate, allowing for highly accurate measurements. These devices are well-suited for various industrial applications, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and HVAC systems, due to their reliability and low maintenance requirements.</p><p>The Vortex Flow Controller Market is poised for significant growth, with expectations to increase at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period.
