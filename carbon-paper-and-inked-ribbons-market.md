<p><p>Carbon paper and inked ribbons are traditional products used for creating copies of written documents. Carbon paper consists of a thin sheet coated with carbon or another ink, placed between two sheets of paper. When pressure is applied, such as by writing or typing, it transfers the ink to the underlying sheet. Inked ribbons, typically found in typewriters and printing devices, are coated with ink and used to imprint characters onto paper.</p><p>The market for carbon paper and inked ribbons is experiencing a notable revival, driven by niche applications in offices, educational institutions, and artistic endeavors.
