<p><p>Noise reduction bone conduction sensors are pivotal in enhancing sound clarity by transmitting vibrations through bones. The market is primarily segmented into two types: piezoelectric and electromagnetic sensors. Piezoelectric sensors utilize materials that generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, effectively capturing vibrational signals. In contrast, electromagnetic sensors rely on magnetic fields and coils to convert vibrations into electrical signals. Both types aim to minimize background noise and improve audio quality in various applications, including hearing aids and communication devices.</p></p>
