<p><ul><li>Clipless Pedals</li><li>Flat Pedals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle pedals market can be categorized into two main types: clipless pedals and flat pedals. Clipless pedals securely attach the rider's shoes to the pedals, enhancing power transfer and control, making them favored by competitive cyclists and long-distance riders. In contrast, flat pedals provide a larger surface area for shoe contact, allowing for easier foot placement and removal, making them popular among casual riders and mountain bikers who value versatility and quick dismounts.</p></p>
