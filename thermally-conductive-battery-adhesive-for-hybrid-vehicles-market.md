<p><p>Thermally conductive battery adhesives for hybrid vehicles are designed to enhance thermal management while securely bonding battery components. The market is primarily divided into gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers provide effective thermal conductivity while filling spaces between surfaces, ensuring even heat distribution. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, offer robust bonding strength and durability, which is crucial for the structural integrity of battery systems. Both types are essential for optimizing performance and safety in hybrid vehicle applications.</p></p>
