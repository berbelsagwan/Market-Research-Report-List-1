<p><strong>The Optical Rotary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical rotary encoders market is primarily divided into two types: incremental and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders generate signals based on movement, providing relative position information, ideal for speed control and motion detection. In contrast, absolute encoders deliver a unique code for each shaft position, ensuring precise location data even during power loss. Both types are crucial in industrial automation, robotics, and automotive applications, facilitating accurate control and feedback in various systems while enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
