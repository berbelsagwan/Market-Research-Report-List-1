<p><p>Flat stove market types include Deep Well Type and Side Suction Type, each catering to distinct cooking requirements. The Deep Well Type features a recessed cooking area that allows for even heat distribution, ideal for pot-based cooking and slow simmering. In contrast, the Side Suction Type integrates a lateral intake system, offering efficient heat management and quick heating, making it suitable for frying and stir-frying. Both types aim to enhance cooking efficiency and user convenience in various culinary applications.</p></p>
