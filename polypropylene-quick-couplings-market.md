<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Elbow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polypropylene Quick Couplings Market is categorized into two primary types: straight couplings and elbow couplings. Straight couplings are designed for direct connections between two fixed pipes, facilitating a straight flow path. In contrast, elbow couplings allow for directional changes in piping systems, providing flexibility in layout design. Both types are essential for various applications in plumbing, irrigation, and industrial systems, offering efficient, leak-free connections while being lightweight and resistant to chemical corrosion.</p></p>
