<p><p>The non-contact temperature market includes infrared temperature sensors and fiber optic temperature sensors. Infrared sensors detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, allowing for rapid and precise temperature measurements without physical contact, making them ideal for various applications, including industrial and medical settings. Fiber optic temperature sensors, on the other hand, utilize fiber optic cables to measure temperature changes through light transmission, offering high accuracy and resistance to electromagnetic interference. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in temperature monitoring across diverse industries.</p></p>
