<p><p>Continuous grain dryers play a vital role in grain storage and processing, categorizing into two main types: horizontal and vertical continuous grain dryers. Horizontal continuous grain dryers feature a robust design that allows grain to move through a horizontal chamber, providing efficient drying with uniform airflow. In contrast, vertical continuous grain dryers utilize a vertical column that processes grain in a continuous flow, optimizing space and energy use. Both types ensure effective moisture removal, enhancing grain quality and shelf life.</p></p>
