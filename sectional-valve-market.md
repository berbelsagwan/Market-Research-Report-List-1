<p><p>The sectional valve market includes sectional directional valves and proportional directional valves, which serve distinct functions in hydraulic systems. Sectional directional valves control the flow and direction of hydraulic fluid, allowing for precise movement of machinery components. Proportional directional valves, on the other hand, offer variable control over fluid flow and pressure, enabling smoother operation and enhanced responsiveness. Both types are crucial in various applications, such as industrial machinery and mobile equipment, improving efficiency and performance in hydraulic systems.</p></p>
