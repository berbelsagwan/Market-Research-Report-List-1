<p><p>Formed in Place Foam Gaskets (FIPFG) refers to a sealing technology where liquid foam materials are applied directly to surfaces during manufacturing. This includes three main market types: Liquid Foam Gaskets, which expand upon application for customized sealing; Thixotropic Foam Gaskets, characterized by their shear-thinning properties, allowing for easy application that thickens post-application; and Compact Gaskets, which provide a solid, durable barrier with minimal compression set, ensuring long-lasting performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
