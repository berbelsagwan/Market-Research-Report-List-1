<p><p>Perforated packaging refers to a specialized type of packaging that features small holes or perforations, allowing for ventilation while maintaining the integrity of the enclosed product. This type of packaging is primarily used for perishable goods such as fruits, vegetables, and meats, as it helps extend shelf life by facilitating air circulation. The perforated design also reduces condensation, preventing spoilage and maintaining product quality.</p><p>The Perforated Packaging Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand from the food and beverage sector, where maintaining freshness and quality is vital.
