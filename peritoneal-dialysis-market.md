<p><p>Peritoneal Dialysis (PD) is a renal replacement therapy that utilizes the peritoneal membrane in the abdomen as a medium for waste removal from the bloodstream. It involves filling the abdominal cavity with a dialysate solution that absorbs toxins and excess fluids, which are then drained away. This method is increasingly becoming popular due to its convenience and flexibility compared to traditional hemodialysis, allowing patients to conduct the procedure in the comfort of their homes.</p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an increasing prevalence of end-stage renal disease (ESRD), rising awareness of home-based dialysis solutions, and advancements in PD technology. Additionally, supportive government initiatives and increased investments in healthcare infrastructure are contributing to the market's expansion. </p><p>Emerging trends include the development of biocompatible dialysis solutions, innovative PD devices, and the incorporation of telehealth services to improve patient management. Moreover, the growing geriatric population and the rising incidence of diabetes are likely to further propel demand for peritoneal dialysis treatments, making it a crucial area of focus in renal care.</p></p>
