<p><p>The research slide scanner market is segmented primarily into two types: brightfield and fluorescence. Brightfield scanners utilize transmitted light to visualize stained tissue samples, making them suitable for histopathology and educational purposes. In contrast, fluorescence scanners employ specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescently labeled samples, enabling the detection of specific proteins and cellular components. Both types cater to various applications in clinical diagnostics, research, and education, enabling enhanced imaging capabilities and data analysis in the life sciences field.</p></p>
