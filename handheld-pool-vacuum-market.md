<p><p>The handheld pool vacuum market is segmented into two main types: corded and cordless handheld vacuums. Corded handheld pool vacuums are powered by electricity, offering consistent suction and performance as they are connected to a power source, making them suitable for larger pools. This makes them ideal for smaller pools or quick clean-ups.</p></p>
