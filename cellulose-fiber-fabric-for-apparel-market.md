<p><p>Cellulose fiber fabric for apparel encompasses various types, including rayon and cuprammonium rayon. Rayon, derived from natural cellulose, offers a silk-like feel, breathability, and drapes elegantly, making it popular in garments. Cuprammonium rayon, a variation, involves dissolving cellulose in a cuprammonium solution, resulting in a softer, more absorbent fabric. Both types are valued for their comfort and versatility, allowing for a wide range of clothing styles while being biodegradable and more environmentally sustainable compared to synthetic fibers.</p></p>
