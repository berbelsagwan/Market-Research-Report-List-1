<p><p>The endotoxin testing system market comprises several types, including turbidimetric assays that measure light scattering to detect endotoxins, and chromogenic assays that utilize color changes to indicate endotoxin presence. Rabbit Factor C (rFC) assays involve recombinant proteins for sensitive detection, while gel clot assays form a gel in the presence of endotoxins, signaling contamination. Other methods may include techniques like ELISA and molecular assays. Each type offers specific advantages in sensitivity, speed, and ease of use for ensuring pharmaceutical safety.</p></p>
