<p><p>Distribution software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based distribution software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and easier maintenance. In contrast, on-premises distribution software requires installation on local servers, providing greater control over data and potentially enhanced security but often involves higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance responsibilities. Each option suits different business needs based on budget, scalability, and control preferences.</p></p>
