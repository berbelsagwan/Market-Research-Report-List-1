<p><p>The Air Lubrication System market mainly comprises two types: Air Films and Microbubbles. Air Films involve the creation of a thin layer of air between the hull and water, reducing drag and enhancing fuel efficiency. Microbubbles utilize tiny air bubbles to create a low-friction layer, further minimizing resistance during movement through water. Both types aim to improve vessel performance, lower operational costs, and reduce environmental impact by decreasing fuel consumption and emissions in maritime applications.</p></p>
