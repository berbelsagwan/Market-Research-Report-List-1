<p><strong>The Disposable Lighters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flint Lighters</li><li>Electronic Lighters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable lighter market is primarily divided into two types: flint lighters and electronic lighters. Flint lighters use a metal striker and flint to produce a spark that ignites fuel, offering affordability and reliability, making them popular for everyday use. In contrast, electronic lighters utilize a battery-operated ignition system, providing a flameless and wind-resistant alternative. Electronic lighters often feature added functionalities, like USB rechargeability, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers and those seeking innovation in their lighter choice.</p></p>
