Single drill bits feature a single PDC cutter for efficient drilling in softer formations. Double drill bits incorporate two PDC cutters, enhancing drilling performance in medium-hard formations. Three-drill bits, equipped with three cutters, provide superior efficiency and durability in hard rock conditions. Each type is tailored to specific geological challenges, optimizing performance and cost-effectiveness in drilling operations.</p></p>
