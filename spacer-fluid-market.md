<p><strong>The Spacer Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscous Spacer</li><li>Turbulent Spacer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spacer fluid market is primarily categorized into viscous spacers and turbulent spacers. Viscous spacers are designed to create a thick, gel-like barrier that helps in the effective separation of fluids during operations, enhancing displacement efficiency. In contrast, turbulent spacers are engineered for high-flow conditions, promoting better mixing and reduced viscosity. Both types play critical roles in various applications, ensuring optimal performance in oil and gas extraction, drilling, and other fluid management processes within the industry.</p></p>
