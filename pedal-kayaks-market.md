<p><ul><li>Push Pedal</li><li>Rotational Pedal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pedal kayaks are categorized into two main types based on their propulsion mechanisms: push pedal and rotational pedal. Push pedal systems operate with a straightforward forward-and-backward motion, resembling a bicycle, which allows for efficient and straightforward pedaling. In contrast, rotational pedal systems involve a circular pedaling motion, offering enhanced maneuverability and speed. Both types cater to different preferences in kayaking experiences, appealing to recreational paddlers and those seeking advanced performance on the water, ultimately expanding the market for pedal-driven watercraft.</p></p>
