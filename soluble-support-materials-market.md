Additionally, innovations in material formulations are enhancing the performance and compatibility of support materials with diverse printing technologies, such as fused deposition modeling (FDM) and stereolithography (SLA). <p><ul><li>Strong Water Solubility</li><li>Medium Water Solubility</li><li>Weak Water Solubility</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble support materials market is categorized based on the solubility level in water: strong, medium, and weak. Strong water solubility materials dissolve readily, making them suitable for complex prints with intricate geometries, providing easy removal after the printing process. Medium solubility materials offer balanced performance, dissolving effectively while retaining some structural integrity during printing. Weak water solubility materials are less soluble, providing support for simpler structures that require minimal post-processing, catering to specific application needs in 3D printing.</p></p>
