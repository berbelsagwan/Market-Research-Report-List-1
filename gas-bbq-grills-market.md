<p><strong>The Gas BBQ Grills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid propane (LP) BBQ Grills</li><li>Natural Gas (NG) BBQ Grills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas BBQ grills market primarily consists of two types: Liquid Propane (LP) BBQ grills and Natural Gas (NG) BBQ grills. LP BBQ grills use portable propane tanks, offering flexibility for outdoor cooking but requiring regular refills. In contrast, NG BBQ grills are directly connected to a natural gas line, providing a continuous fuel supply, making them convenient for prolonged use. Both types offer efficient cooking options, with preferences often depending on user needs, cooking habits, and available gas sources.</p></p>
