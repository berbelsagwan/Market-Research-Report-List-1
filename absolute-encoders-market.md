<p><ul><li>Solid Shaft</li><li>Hollow Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absolute encoder market is primarily divided into two types based on shaft design: solid shaft and hollow shaft. Solid shaft encoders feature a single, sturdy shaft that connects directly to the motor, offering reliable performance in compact spaces. In contrast, hollow shaft encoders have a central opening, allowing for easy installation over existing shafts, accommodating various mechanical setups. Both types are crucial for precise position sensing in industrial applications, robotics, and automation, catering to different installation requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
