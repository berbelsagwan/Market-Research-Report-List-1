<p><ul><li>Fluid Acrylics</li><li>Heavy-Body Acrylics</li><li>Slow-Drying Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-commercial acrylic paints market includes several key types. Fluid acrylics are characterized by their thin viscosity, making them ideal for pouring, glazing, and detailed work. Heavy-body acrylics are thicker and provide a rich texture, suitable for impasto techniques and three-dimensional effects. Slow-drying agents are additives that extend the drying time of acrylics, allowing for more blending and manipulation of colors. Together, these products cater to various artistic techniques and preferences, enhancing the creative possibilities for artists.</p></p>
