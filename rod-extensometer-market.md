<p><strong>The Rod Extensometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point Rod Extensometer</li><li>Multipoint Rod Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rod extensometer market is segmented into single point and multipoint extensometers, each serving distinct applications in geotechnical and structural monitoring. A single point rod extensometer measures displacement at a single location, providing precise data for point-specific studies. In contrast, a multipoint rod extensometer features multiple measurement points along a single rod, enabling comprehensive displacement assessment over a larger area. These tools are essential for monitoring soil deformation, structural integrity, and assessing stability in various engineering projects.</p></p>
