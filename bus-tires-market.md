<p><p>The bus tires market primarily consists of two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layered construction with diagonal plies, offering durability and stability, making them suitable for short-distance trips. In contrast, radial tires utilize a radial ply design, enhancing fuel efficiency and better traction, making them ideal for long-distance travel. Each tire type serves specific operational needs, impacting performance, lifespan, and overall cost-effectiveness for bus operators, thus influencing market dynamics and consumer preferences.</p></p>
