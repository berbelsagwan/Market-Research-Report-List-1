<p><p>The AiP (Antenna in Package) module market encompasses several technologies, including LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic), HDI (High-Density Interconnect), and FOWLP (Fan-Out Wafer Level Packaging). LTCC offers robust performance for high-frequency applications with integrated passive components. HDI provides compact designs with enhanced wiring density, suitable for advanced electronics. FOWLP utilizes a fan-out structure to improve packaging efficiency and thermal performance, enabling miniaturization while maintaining functionality. Each type caters to specific requirements in the evolving semiconductor landscape.</p></p>
