<p><p>The hardshell jacket market includes two primary types: three-layer shells and durable water repellent (DWR) coatings. Three-layer shells consist of an outer fabric, a waterproof membrane, and an inner lining, providing superior protection against harsh weather while maintaining breathability. DWR coatings enhance the water resistance of fabrics, allowing for improved water repellency and durability. Both types aim to offer outdoor enthusiasts reliable protection in varying conditions, promoting comfort and performance during activities like hiking and skiing.</p></p>
