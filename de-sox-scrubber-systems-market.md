<p><p>De-SOx scrubber systems are pollution control technologies used to reduce sulfur oxide emissions from ships and industrial processes. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop**: Utilizes seawater for scrubbing, discharging treated water back into the ocean. </p><p>2. **Closed Loop**: Employs a chemical solution that is recirculated, with waste products being disposed of separately. </p><p>3. **Hybrid**: Combines both open and closed loop systems, allowing flexibility depending on regulations and operational conditions.</p></p>
